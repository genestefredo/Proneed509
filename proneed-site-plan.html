Overview

Purpose

- Promoting Nutritional Care of Children.
- Provide the necessary financial support for the elementary education of children.
- Be at the service of human development with and for the most disadvantaged population.

Audience

Proneed works in the field of nutrition and education;
So that Children, Nursing Mother and Pregnant Women should get nutritional supplements according the need and people in all area a city in Haiti have a more accessible school environment.
For more visible access visit our Facebook page on this webpage, Instagram and our e-mail: proneed509@gmail.com for more information.

We provide nutritional supplements to children in 9 different areas around the country using local leaders and local supplements, focusing on the first 1,000 days of life. Once a child has been screened and identified as undernourished according to World Health Organization growth charts, they are enrolled in our program.
